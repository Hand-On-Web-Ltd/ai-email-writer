# AI Email Writer ✉️

Turn bullet points into polished professional emails. Pick an email type, jot down your key points, and get a ready-to-send email in seconds.

No API key needed. No sign-up. Everything runs in your browser.

## Email Types

- **Follow-up** — after a meeting or call
- **Cold outreach** — reaching out to someone new
- **Thank you** — showing appreciation
- **Complaint** — professional but firm
- **Introduction** — introducing yourself or your business
- **Meeting request** — asking for a meeting

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Pick the email type
3. Enter your bullet points (one per line)
4. Fill in the recipient name and your name
5. Click "Generate Email"
6. Copy the result

## How It Works

This isn't calling ChatGPT or any external API. It uses built-in templates and fills in your content. The result is a clean, professional email based on your bullet points.

It won't win a writing award, but it'll save you 10 minutes every time you need to write a tricky email.

## Tech

- Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- No external APIs or dependencies
- Works offline
